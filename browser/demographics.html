Demography of the Wikimedia development community

This study is based in the information obtained from the Git, Bugzilla and Mailman from Wikiemdia. The charts below show information about the number of contributors (X axis) every six months (Y axis).

Mailing list aging show the number of forum members joining the forum each six months that are still active

Horizontal bars represent periods of six months where the 0 bar shows information of the last six months.

Big bars on top would mean that old contributors are still active. Big bars at the bottom would mean that new contributors are being retained.

Mailing list birth shows the number of newcomers joining the forum eachs six months

Horizontal bars represent periods of six months where the 0 bar shows information of the last six months.

Biggers bars on top would mean that your community attracted more people before. Bigger bars at the bottom would mean that your community is attracting more people now.
